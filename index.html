Biography

Who I Am

Minister-in-formation at Atlantic School of Theology, Halifax, Nova Scotia. My theology is shaped by liberation, process, and contemplative traditions.

Ministry

Student minister at Hope United Church, Halifax. I preach, lead worship, and build liturgical tools for congregations. Grounded in a theology of liberation — God's power as invitation, not coercion.

Academic Formation

  • MDiv candidate, Atlantic School of Theology
  • Philosophy & classical languages
  • Mathematics & economics

Theological Commitments

  • Scripture as living encounter

Craft & Method
